Fuelling innovation

Innovation is at the centre of everything we do - from our fuels, to our stations, and our service offerings. Here are a few things we’ve achieved over the years in Singapore:

1986

First convenience store in a service station at Esso West Coast

1988

First fuel payment card

1989

First to offer fresh food such as hot pastries at Esso West Coast

2001

Only fuel brand to offer contactless payment at the pump with Speedpass

2002

The Smiles loyalty program is introduced

2004

Opening of ExxonMobil-NTUC FairPrice Pilot Alliance sites

2006

FairPrice Xpress & Cheers stores open at Esso service stations

2007

First bigger than usual convenience store in Singapore at Esso Upper Changi North

2015

A new look for our service stations, along with improved Synergy petrol formulations

Powering McLaren-Honda

For over 20 years, we’ve partnered with McLaren, now McLaren-Honda, to help push the boundaries of performance on the Grand Prix circuit.

The technical team that has developed this season’s fuel has been working closely with the Honda team to optimise product performance for the MP4-30 since late 2013.

The race fuel is developed in the same technology centre in Paulsboro, New Jersey, USA, as our Esso Synergy fuel formulations.

It is this culture of driving innovation from the race to the road that continues to help you get more out of your journeys.
