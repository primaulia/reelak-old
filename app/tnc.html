Terms and Conditions of ExxonMobil Asia Pacific Pte. Ltd. (EMAPPL)'s 2015 'Synergy Race Off' ("Competition").

Eligibility

  1. This Competition is open to all members of the public (no age limit).

Competition Period

  1. The Competition period is from 5 to 6 September 2015 at ION Basement 4 and 12 to 13 September 2015 at ION2 ("Preliminary Rounds"), with the finals held on 16 September 2015 at a venue to be advised ("Finale Event").

Mechanics

  1. All participants will have to register with their full particulars (Name, NRIC / passport number, contact number, e-mail address and residency details) before racing in the simulators in the Preliminary Rounds.
  2. Visitors to Singapore are eligible to participate. To qualify as one of the top 10 finalists, he or she will need to be present during the Finale Event on 16 September, which will be held in Singapore.
  3. Each participant will get one attempt for one lap on a simulator, set at "Amateur" mode, in the Preliminary Rounds. Each participant will be given a maximum of three (3) minutes to finish one lap on the simulator. Once the time is up, the simulator will be stopped.
  4. Participants who produce a receipt of Synergy fuel purchase at an Esso station dated 1 August 2015 and later will be entitled to another attempt on the simulator, immediately following his or her first attempt. Each receipt may only be used once to redeem a second attempt on the simulator. Each participant will be limited to two attempts on the simulator with each registration.
  5. In the event of repeat attempts, only the participants' fastest timing will be considered for purpose of selection for the Finale Event. One participant may only hold one place in the leaderboard.
  6. A total of ten (10) participants, with the fastest timings at the simulators in the Preliminary Rounds, will be selected as the finalists ("Finalists"). The Finalists will be given a chance to race against Jenson Button in a simulator at the Finale Event on 16 September 2015.
  7. Each Finalist will be contacted by EMAPPL and/or its appointed contractors via telephone and email using the contact information provided upon registration. In the event that EMAPPL and/or its appointed contractors is/are unable to contact any Finalist for any reason through this first phone call, EMAPPL and/or its appointed contractors will make a further two (2) attempts to contact the Finalist during office hours (9.00am – 6.00pm) of the same day that the first call was made. In the event that all such attempts are unsuccessful, EMAPPL reserves the right to select the next eligible participant as a Finalist, based on the next fastest timing.
  8. If any Finalist does not confirm their attendance and participation at the Finale Event on the 16 September 2015 by 15 September 2015 at 12:00pm, their spot as a Finalist will be dealt with at EMAPPL's sole discretion.
  9. The preliminary results of the Preliminary Rounds will be published on the Esso website by 15 September 2015.
  10. Prizes for the Finalists will be awarded based on the recorded timings of the simulator races at the Finale Event on the 16 September 2015. In the event of any dispute, EMAPPL reserves the right to determine the order of winners at its sole discretion.
  11. By registering or participating in this Competition, participants hereby consent to grant EMAPPL and/or its appointed contractors the right to use the race results, his/her performance, name, photograph, video recording, quotes, picture, likeness, interview, biography and other indications of his/her identity in EMAPPL's promotional or publicity materials (whether printed, electronic, or otherwise) as they reasonably deem fit, including, without limitation, in such publication mentioned in Clause 11 above.
  12. Award of all prizes in this Competition is conditional upon such consent being granted as stated in Clause 13 above, and any Finalist who does not so consent (upon notification by EMAPPL and/or its appointed contractors via telephone and email that he/she is a Finalist pursuant to Clause 9 above) shall have his/her spot as a Finalist and any subsequent award of prize(s) forfeited and EMAPPL shall have the right to select the next fastest participant as a Finalist and award these prize(s) to him/her instead.
  13. EMAPPL's decisions on all matters relating to this Competition (including, without limitation, the selection of Finalists) shall be final and binding. EMAPPL will not enter into any correspondence or communication with any party in relation to such decisions.

Prizes

  1. There will be a total of ten (10) winners who will get a chance to race against Jenson Button at the Finale Event on 16 September 2015 and to win other prizes to be determined at the sole discretion of EMAPPL. Winners will be contacted by EMAPPL.
  2. All prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.

Other Terms and Conditions

  1. Visitors to the event area may be captured in photography and/or video recording that will be carried out during the event. EMAPPL reserved the right to utilize these photographs and video recordings taken for promotional and publicity purposes.
  2. EMAPPL shall not be responsible or liable for any medical condition, injury, claim, loss or damage whatsoever arising out of or in connection with the Competition or the prizes awarded during the Competition.
  3. For enquiries, please contact the Smiles Driver Rewards Customer Service Centre at 1800-300-8888, Monday to Friday (except public holidays), from 9:00am to 6:00pm.
  4. The terms and conditions for this Competition may be revised from time to time at EMAPPL's sole discretion without prior notice. Please check www.esso.com.sg/synergyraceoff for the latest terms and conditions.
